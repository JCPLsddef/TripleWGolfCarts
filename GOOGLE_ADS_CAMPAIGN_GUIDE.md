## 7. BIDDING STRATEGY

### Day 1 Start: Manual CPC with Enhanced CPC

**Why:** With only $10/day budget and no conversion data, Manual CPC gives you control while Enhanced CPC helps optimize bids based on conversion likelihood.

**Settings:**
- **Bidding:** Manual CPC
- **Enhanced CPC:** ON
- **Starting Max CPC Bids:**
  - Ad Group 1 (Core Local): $3.00
  - Ad Group 2 (Events): $3.50
  - Ad Group 3 (Brand): $1.50

**Daily Monitoring (Days 1-14):**
- Check Search Impression Share (lost due to budget)
- If losing 50%+ impressions due to budget: Lower bids by $0.50
- If losing <20% impressions: Hold steady
- If not getting clicks: Increase bids by $0.50 (max $5.00)

---

### When to Switch: Maximize Conversions

**Timing:** After 30-50 conversions (approximately 30-60 days with $10/day)

**Why:** Maximize Conversions needs conversion data to work. With low budget, this takes time.

**Settings:**
- **Bidding:** Maximize Conversions
- **Target CPA:** DO NOT SET initially (let it learn)
- **After 50+ conversions:** Add tCPA of $30

**How to Switch:**
1. Go to Campaign Settings â†’ Bidding
2. Change to "Maximize Conversions"
3. Leave Target CPA blank
4. Monitor daily for 7 days
5. If average CPL is <$25: Hold steady
6. If average CPL is $30-$40: Add tCPA of $30
7. If average CPL is >$40: Switch back to Manual CPC or pause low-performing keywords

---

### Budget Management

**If Budget is Consistently Maxed Out:**
- Review Search Terms daily
- Add more negative keywords aggressively
- Pause lowest-performing keywords (high CPC, low conversion rate)
- Consider lowering bids to stretch budget

**If Budget is NOT Being Spent:**
- Increase bids gradually ($0.50 increments)
- Expand to Phrase Match more aggressively
- Add more keywords from "Related Searches" in Google
- Check if ads are showing (low Quality Score issue?)

## FINAL TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Be Patient:** With $10/day, expect slow ramp-up. First conversion may take 5-10 days.

2. **Prioritize Phone Calls:** They convert faster than forms. Make sure call extension is active.

3. **Ruthless on Negatives:** Your $10 budget can't afford wasted clicks. Add negatives daily.

4. **Monitor Search Terms Daily:** This is your #1 optimization lever in the first 2 weeks.

5. **Don't Pause Too Early:** Keywords need 20-30 clicks before you can judge performance.

6. **Quality Score Matters:** If QS is <5, fix it before increasing bids. Better ads = lower CPC.

7. **Mobile Matters:** 60%+ of clicks will be mobile. Ensure your site loads fast and phone number is clickable.

8. **Track Everything:** Conversions, calls, form starts, search terms. More data = better decisions.

9. **Local Intent is King:** Focus on "Tyler TX" and "East Texas" keywords first. Expand slowly.

10. **Test and Iterate:** Try different ad copy, adjust bids, test new keywords. Improve 1% per week = 50% better by year end.
